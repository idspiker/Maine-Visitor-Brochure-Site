Welcome to Maine

The state of Maine has a lower than average population with it only being home to around 1,389,000 people (the national average is 5,763,868) [1][2]. Despite being smaller than the national median, Maine has a lot to offer. The state is beautiful year round and offers many outdoor activities during the warm months. Maine is seeing more and more life science businesses starting up and moving in, and there are already established industries in forestry, agriculture, and aquaculture [3]. The state also prides itself on its clean energy resources, which are always growing due to smart research and innovation [3].

Parks and Outdoor Activities

Maine is home to four national parks and thirty-two state parks [1][2]. If you love the outdoors, Maine is for you. Beautiful scenery abounds, allowing for photography and nature site-seeing. Some parks, such as Acadia National Park, combine mountains and rugged coasts to create a picturesque landscape. If you are more of the active type, Maine has you covered. The parks are full of opportunities for hiking and biking. Whether you are looking for a leisurely stroll or an intense climb there are many options available.
